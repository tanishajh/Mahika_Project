Empowering Home-Based Women Entrepreneurs

Mahika is a digital platform for household women to promote their home-run businesses, receive online orders, and gain financial independence. Currently operating in Mumbai.

Problems We Solve

  • Lack of online visibility for home-run businesses
  • Difficulty in marketing products or services
  • Limited access to a wider customer base
  • Low digital and financial literacy

Our Solution

  • User-friendly website to register and showcase businesses
  • Online tools for order management and payments
  • WhatsApp and email-based inquiry system
  • Community support and knowledge sharing
